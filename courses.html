Book season

Custom:

Everything is customized for you. I don’t have a set program. We’ll talk and I’ll assess the current state of your brand, business or career. Through our work we’ll uncover what might be standing in your way. Then, together we’ll design the best path to achieve your goals.

video:

We’ll use Zoom to meet via video conference. Each session is recorded and provided to you for future reference.

frequency:

Again, everything is custom. We’ll meet as frequently as you need. Some clients need more time between meetings to progress in their work. Others like to meet frequently. Some just reach out “for a quick fix”. We’ll determine what will work best to achieve the results you want.

preparation:

Prior to working together I ask my coaching clients to share 3-5 topics they want to be sure to address. This helps me prepare in advance of our session and focus in on your priorities. Share your website, social channels, anything you think might help me get a clear picture of where you are.

PRICING ($297/hr):

Many coaches with my level of expertise charge $500-$1000+ per session and require long-term commitments. I strive to make my coaching accessible to as many people as possible by keeping my fees low, enabling us to work together more often.
